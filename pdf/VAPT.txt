Q)Authenticity is proving who you are, and integrity is protecting the data from unauthorized changes.

Integrity is about making sure that some piece of data has not been altered from some "reference version". 
Authenticity is a special case of integrity, where the "reference version" is defined as "
whatever it was when it was under control of a specific entity".
